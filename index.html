SUNGKA NATIVE RESTAURANT

"Experience Serenity and Savor Filipino Foods at Sungka Native Restaurant: A Unique Dining Oasis in Cebu"

Calling all fish enthusiasts and aquarists! For you, we have some great news! It is said that looking at fish in an aquarium reduces stress, which in turn decreases blood pressure. It greatly eases nervousness and considerably lowers anxiety.

However, what if theres a restaurant that is constructed around the koi pond?

Imagine dining at a magnificent restaurant with a sizable koi pond as its backdrop. Fish with koi scales glide effortlessly across the water, reflecting the sun with their colorful scales and the splashing water has a soothing sound that calms you.

Sungka is a Filipino word for a popular game in the Philippines. It entails dropping a canoe-shaped board into a hole. Perhaps they call it sungka because they offer a wide range of Filipino cuisines. The restaurant is set away from the bustle of the city in a calm, isolated area. Diners would enjoy the serene and intimate setting for diners.

Indulge their homely tastes of Filipino culinary delights!

Traditional Filipino food with a specialty in fresh seafood may be found on the menu at Dine by the Pond. So definitely, you can enjoy Filipino food cuisine with a unique concept. You are allowed to consume sinigang na baboy, guso, scallops, and pork BBQ. The restaurant has a bar as well, and they can serve up delicious pastries and regional beers. While you are eating, you can feed the fishes and if you are to touch the water, the fishes will kiss your hands. Indeed the place is instagramable and you can take photos anywhere. It's the ideal setting for any kind of date, whether it's with friends, family, or a partner! You can celebrate your special occasions in there and majority of the customers who dine in is when its their birthday and the staff will gladly sing a birthday song for them.

So what are you waiting for? Sungka Native Restaurant is the destination for you, if you are searching for an exceptional and memorable dining experience in Cebu. Come and take in the tranquillity of the koi pond while enjoying the mouthwatering flavors of Filipino food.
